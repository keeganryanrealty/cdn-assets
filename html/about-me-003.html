About

Their guidance has shaped the way I approach negotiation, client relationships, and decision-making—with clarity, strategy, and care at the center of it all.

I believe buying or selling a home shouldn’t feel overwhelming. It should feel personal, purposeful, and built on trust. My job is to help you move forward with confidence—not just into your next chapter, but toward the best future you can imagine.
